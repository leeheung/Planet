PLANET(행성)

A planet is a class of celestial bodies that revolve around a star.
Generally speaking, a planet is smaller than a brown dwarf,
but a celestial body with a diameter of thousands of kilometers or more.
Before the 20th century, only eight planets in the solar system were known,
but at least 200 alien planets are known by 2012 as
the planets outside the solar system can be observed

In general, the planet is said to have been formed by the gathering of dust
and gaseous matter that circulated around the primitive when the nebula ,
which is the origin of some stars , collapsed.
